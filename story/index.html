OUR STORY

Axiom Nut Trading embarked on its journey with the aspiration of introducing the people to the international standard best quality dry fruits and nuts that can satisfy every taste bud around the world. We started off as a subsidiary of Ismail Cashew Exports and N.N Cashew Exports, together make one of the world’s top growing suppliers, distributors and exporters of edible nuts and fruits. We believe that a perfect guidance can take anyone miles ahead. Hence we follow the footsteps of our founders, who are pioneers in the nuts industry with more than 20 years of experience in offering best quality services to their clients.

A good team helps in attaining great results. The support of a rich vendor base helps us in ensuring that we deliver superior quality of products to our customers. We give as much importance to our products as much we give to our customers. Regular quality checks, tie ups with reputed firms and an emphasis on maintaining quality standard of production helps us in ensuring that there is no compromise in the quality of our products. The consistency in the superior quality of our products and the wide range of services we offer, makes us different from the rest of the market. Our client base is our strongest asset. The immense satisfaction of our clients that weattained through our ethical business practices and customer centric approach has helped us gain a respectable position in Indian and GCC markets.

We at Axiom Nut Trading strive hard to fulfill all the needs and requests in the best interest of our client. Hence we bring to you a world of opportunity at your doorsteps, with the sole intention of helping you grow further in your respected venture.

OUR PRODUCTS
Dry Fruits
Almonds

It’s a well know fact that almonds are highly nutritious nut. They are a rich source of vitamin E, calcium, iron and magnesium. Usually connected to higher intellectual level, they help in the development and health of human brain. Grab a few of our delicious almonds every day for a healthy body and mind!

Cashews

The delicately flavoured cashew nut is a favourite between meal and snack. The health benefits of cashews include a healthy heart, strong muscle function, an improved bone and oral health among others. What more? It is a great taste enhancer; make your favorite dishes 10 times more flavourful with just a few topping of our top notch cashews!

Brazil Nuts

Brazil Nuts are the number one food source for selenium, an extremely vital mineral for human immunity. They also help in preventing cancer, as well lifting moods; hence they are an excellent addition to depression diet. The next time you feel down, cheer yourself up with this lip smacking and healthy snack.

Hazel Nuts

Who doesn’t love hazelnut flavour in coffee or chocolates! It’s a versatile nut which can be enjoyed raw, roasted, or as an ingredient in variety of dishes. It also helps in managing diabetes, controlling cholesterol and boosting the functioning of brain. Now this is the best example of healthy can be tasty!

Macadamia Nuts

Native to Australia, these are considered as the world’s finest nuts. And why not, this butterly flavoured nut not only takes care of your taste buds but also of your body. They help in controlling heart diseases, managing weight loss, preventing hai breakage and delaying skin aging. They are what we like to call, a butterly delight.

Walnuts

King of dry fruits, walnut can provide numerous heath benefits at a very mild dose. They have cancer fighting properties; aid in weight loss.Eating just four walnuts a day has been shown to significantly raise blood levels of heart-healthy. Toss them around in salads or deserts and adds a crunchy twist!

Tree Fruits
Apricots

These small dupes, closely related to peaches are known for their ability to treat in digestion, fever, skin diseases and cancers.There versatility can be judged by the wide range of uses they serve. Apart from direct consumption you can use them to make delicious jams, squash, juices and even jellies. They are the perfect blend of beauty and taste.

Dates

Dates have always been known for their healing powers. These sweet and delicious dark fruits are rich in vitamins; they promote digestion, strengthen the nervous system and help in weight gain. Our dates sell like hot cakes. Don’t believe us? Time to try them yourself!

Figs

Considered exotic in many areas around the world, figs are high source of fibre and other essential minerals including magnesium, calcium etc. This eye catching fruit serves the taste buds well .We handpick our figs to make sure, you fall in love with them.

Prunes

A dried form of plum, prunes have acted as a popular digestive remedy for decades. Their health benefits also help prevent obesity and reduce the risk of osteoporosis among others. Add that pinch of extra flavour and nutrition to your daily routine with our special prunes.

Cherries

This bright fruit is not only appealing to the eyes, but also to our taste buds. Bursting with vitamins and antioxidants, they are known for reducing inflammation as well as blood pressure of regularly eaten. The sweet and sour flavours are sure to make you addicted to them!
